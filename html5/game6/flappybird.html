Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to score the highest score.
  • The game ends when the bird hits the pipe or the ground.
  • Pipes height are randomly generated.


Logic Design and Implementation:

  • Write proper logics to make pipe appear at random height.
  • Write simple and proper logics for making the bird fly on the hit of "Space" key.
  • Create variables to hold the score and import images.
  • Write simple logic to draw images.
