Objective:

  • The objective of the game is to successfully arrange the shapes in such a way that they form complete horizontal blocks which are then cleared from the game screen.
  • Shapes are randomly generated and they move downwards until they land up on the ground or on top of another shape block.
  • The downward speed of the shapes increase depending upon the score.
  • The game gets over when the last generated block can not be accomodated successfully.


Logic Design and Implementation:

  • Use suqare objects to generate the different shapes under the shape class.
  • Write proper logics for shape transformations on the hit of "Space" key.
  • Maintain a separate Environment class which changes the color of those particular nodes that coincide with the shape that lands either on the floor or on top of another shape block.
  • Clear the horizontal blocks without any gaps using simple logic.
  • Create variables to hold the score and display the next shape and the shape that's put on hold.
  • Create logics to determine when the game is over and display it on the screen.
